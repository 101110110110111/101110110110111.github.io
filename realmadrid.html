Background

Real Madrid Club de Fútbol, simply known as just Real Madrid, is a pro Spanish football club in Madrid. The club which traditionally has a white home kit, was founded on 6 March 1902. The title real along with the royal crown which appears on their emblem was assigned to them by King Alfonso XIII in 1920.

Real Madrid has been owned and operated by its members all throughout their history. After being dubbed the most valuable football club in the world, their estimated worth is set to be around the $1.46 billion mark. Which explains why they are one of the most widely supported teams in the world.

During the 1950s Real Madrid established themselves as a major force in world football. In a spam of seven years they dominated competitions both domestically and internationally, by winning the European Cup five consecutive times in seven finals appearances. During that time they also managed to win the Spanish League title five times too.

From there Los Blancos would go on to win a total of 96 major trophies and counting in their history. Their trophy cabinet includes 66 domestic titles: a record 34 La Liga titles, 19x Copa Del Rey Champions, 11 Supercopas de España, an Eva Duarte Cup, and a Copa de La Liga. In Europe and beyond, Real Madrid holds the record with 26 trophies conquered: 13 UEFA Champions League titles, two UEFA Cups, four UEFA Super Cups, and seven Club World Championships.

On 11 December 2000, Real Madrid was recognized by FIFA as the FIFA Club of the 20th Century.
